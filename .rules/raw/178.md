## Spell Types

- Spells are defined by two primary types: mana spells and physical spells.
- Mana spells affect mental, spiritual, or magical targets, including life force and emotions.
- Mana spells are the only spells that can affect astral forms.
- Physical spells manipulate physical properties.
- Physical spells are the only spell type that can affect non-living objects.

## Spell Categories

- Spells are grouped into five functional categories: combat, detection, health, illusion, and manipulation.
- Combat spells channel destructive energy directly into a target.
- Combat spells damage a target from within.
- Combat spells bypass non-magical armor.
- Detection spells grant the subject enhanced senses or entirely new senses.
- Health spells affect the condition or performance of a living body.
- Illusion spells deceive the senses.
- Manipulation spells transform, animate, or control matter and energy.

## Spell Durations

- Instant spells take effect and vanish in the same action.
- Sustained spells maintain their effect while the caster concentrates.
- Permanent spells become permanent after being sustained for a required period.
- While sustaining a spell, the caster takes a +2 target number penalty to tests.

## Simultaneous Sustained Spells

- A caster can simultaneously sustain a number of spells up to their Sorcery Skill rating.
