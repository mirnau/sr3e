## Combat Turn Pool Refresh

- At the beginning of each Combat Turn, dice pools refresh to their full value.

## Combat Phase Timing

- A character acts when combat reaches their Initiative Score within an Initiative Pass.
- Initiative Score changes from damage can affect later Initiative Passes or acting order in the same Combat Turn.

## Free Actions

- A magician can allocate Spell Defense dice at the beginning of any character's Combat Phase if the magician has already had their first action of the turn.

## Spell Targeting Barriers

- Spellcasting requires clear line of sight to the target.
- Transparent barriers, such as glass, do not block mana spells.
- Transparent barriers, such as glass, do not block most physical spells.
- Transparent barriers block Elemental Manipulation spells.
- Elemental Manipulation spells are treated as ranged attacks.
