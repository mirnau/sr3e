## Levitate

- Levitate movement speed equals the caster's Magic Attribute multiplied by Sorcery Test successes, in meters per Combat Turn.
- The number of successes used for this calculation is capped by the spell's Force.
- The difficulty of levitating a target increases in weight-class steps: +1 target number per full 100kg of target mass.

## Magic Fingers

- Magic Fingers' effective Strength and Quickness equal the number of Sorcery Test successes.
- The effective Strength and Quickness are capped by the spell's Force.
