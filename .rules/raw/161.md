## Noticing Spellcasting

- Casting a spell with a spell fetish is more discreet.
- Observers attempting to notice magic being performed take a -1 target number modifier when the spell uses a fetish.

## Drain

- Drain is fatigue or injury suffered from channeling mana.
- Each spell the magician sustains adds +2 to the Drain Power for any new magic performed.
- Drain that becomes Deadly Physical can lead to permanent Magic loss checks.
- After Deadly Physical Drain, roll 2D6; if the result is less than or equal to the caster's current Magic Attribute, reduce Magic by 1.
- If Magic reaches 0, the caster burns out and becomes mundane permanently.
