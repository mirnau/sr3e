## Foci

- A focus is a magical item that assists a magician or adept with magical skills or physical abilities.
- A focus looks mundane to mundane observers, but has an astral presence.
- Foci are acquired from talismongers.

## Bonding and Activation

- A focus must be bonded to its owner before use.
- Bonding a focus requires a ritual lasting hours equal to the focus's Force.
- Bonding requires Good Karma unless a focus type explicitly requires no Karma to bond.
- Activating a bonded focus requires a Simple Action.
- A character can have active foci at one time up to their Intelligence rating.
- Dropping a focus or having it snatched away immediately deactivates it.

## Astral Properties

- Active foci are dual-natured.
- Active foci retain the owner's astral signature.
- An active focus can be used to track its owner through astral space.

## Spell Foci

- A spell focus provides extra dice equal to its Force for Sorcery tests or Drain Resistance tests.
- An expendable spell focus is a one-use item and requires no Karma to bond.
- A specific spell focus is bonded to one specific spell.
- A spell category focus aids all spells in one spell category.
- A spell category focus can be used for Spell Defense or Dispelling.

## Spirit Foci

- A spirit focus provides extra dice equal to its Force for summoning, controlling, or banishing one specific type of spirit.

## Power Foci

- A power focus increases the owner's Magic Attribute by its Force for determining Drain type.
- A power focus increases the owner's Magic Attribute by its Force for determining maximum spirit Force.
- A power focus provides dice equal to its Force for Sorcery, Conjuring, Drain, or Spell Defense.

## Sustaining Foci

- A sustaining focus maintains a sustained spell without the caster's concentration.
- A sustaining focus can sustain a spell only if the spell's Force is less than or equal to the focus's Force.
- Casting a spell into a sustaining focus is an Exclusive Complex Action.

## Weapon Foci

- A weapon focus is a magical melee weapon.
- A weapon focus adds its Force to the owner's combat skill.
- A weapon focus can strike spirits with Immunity to Normal Weapons.
- A weapon focus follows its owner into astral space during astral projection.

## Bonding Costs and Legality

- Expendable spell focus bonding cost: 0 Good Karma.
- Specific spell focus bonding cost: 1 x Force Good Karma.
- Spirit focus bonding cost: 2 x Force Good Karma.
- Spell category focus bonding cost: 3 x Force Good Karma.
- Power focus bonding cost: 5 x Force Good Karma.
- Sustaining focus bonding cost: 1 x Force Good Karma.
- Weapon focus bonding cost: (3 + Reach) x Force Good Karma.
- Any focus of Force 3 or higher is restricted.
- A restricted focus has Legality Code `(8 - Force)P-T`.
