## Illusion Spells

- Illusion spells deceive senses.
- Mana-based illusions affect the mind.
- Physical illusions create actual sensory input.
- Physical illusions can fool technological sensors.

## Confusion

- Confusion (Chaos) applies a +1 target number modifier to all of the target's tests per Sorcery Test success.
- The modifier cannot exceed the spell's Force.
