## Spell Resistance

- A living unwilling target rolls a Resistance Test.
- The Resistance Test uses the targeted attribute.
- The Resistance Test target number equals the spell's Force.
- Non-living objects do not receive a Resistance Test.

## Spell Effect

- Compare Sorcery Test successes to Resistance Test successes.
- If the caster has more successes, the spell takes effect.
- Net successes determine the spell effect's potency.
- If the caster has no Sorcery Test successes, the spell fails, but Drain Resistance still occurs.
- For damaging combat spells, every two net successes increase the Damage Level by one stage.
- Damage cannot be staged beyond Deadly.
- If resistance staging reduces Damage Level below Light, no damage is inflicted.

## Elemental Manipulation Staging

- Elemental manipulations are treated as ranged attacks for staging.
- The caster stages elemental manipulation damage up by one Damage Level for every two Sorcery Test successes.
- The target stages elemental manipulation damage down by one Damage Level for every two Damage Resistance Test successes.

## Spell Defense Timing

- A magician can allocate Spell Defense dice at the beginning of any character's Combat Phase if the magician has already had their first action of the turn.

## Drain Resistance

- After casting, the caster resists Drain from channeling mana.
- In combat, Drain Resistance is the final step of the spellcasting sequence and occurs immediately after spell effect is determined during the caster's Combat Phase.
- Drain Resistance uses Willpower plus any remaining Spell Pool dice.
- Drain Resistance target number equals half the spell's Force, rounded down, plus the spell's specific Drain modifier.
- Background count can modify Drain Resistance target number.
- Every two Drain Resistance successes reduce Drain damage by one level.
- If the spell's Force exceeds the caster's Magic Attribute, Drain damage is Physical.
- If the spell's Force does not exceed the caster's Magic Attribute, Drain damage is Stun.
- If the caster is astrally projecting, Drain damage is Physical.
- Physical or Stun damage from Drain immediately reduces the caster's Initiative Score by the corresponding injury modifier.
