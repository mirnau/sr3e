## Manipulation Spells

- Manipulation spells transform, animate, or control matter and energy.
- Control manipulations affect the thoughts and actions of living beings.
- Elemental manipulations create matter or energy, such as fire or acid, to cause damage.
- Elemental manipulations are treated as normal ranged attacks.
- Elemental manipulations use a base target number of 4 and can be dodged.
- Impact armor provides half its normal protection, rounded down, against elemental manipulation effects.
- Telekinetic manipulations exert magical force to move or manipulate objects.
- Transformation manipulations transform or transmute matter and energy.

## Silence

- Silence applies a +1 Hearing target number modifier per Sorcery Test success.
- The modifier cannot exceed the spell's Force.
