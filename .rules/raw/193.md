## Health Spells

- Health spells affect a living body's condition or performance.
- Health spell effects can include healing injuries, purging toxins, or modifying attributes.

## Attribute Modification

- Increase and Decrease Attribute spells change the target Attribute by 1 point per 2 Sorcery Test successes.
- The Attribute change cannot exceed the spell's Force.
- Increase Reflexes and similar Reaction-scaling spells follow the same 1 point per 2 successes formula, capped by Force.
