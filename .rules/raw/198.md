## Shadow

- Shadow applies a +1 Visibility target number modifier per Sorcery Test success.
- The modifier is capped by whichever is lower: 8, or the spell's Force.
- Formula: modifier = min(successes, 8, Force).

## Physical and Astral Barrier

- One Sorcery Test success sets the Barrier Rating equal to the spell's Force.
- Every 2 additional net successes beyond the first increase the Barrier Rating by 1.
- Formula: rating = Force + floor((net successes - 1) / 2).

## Ice Sheet

- Ice Sheet applies a +1 target number modifier to avoid falling for every 2 Sorcery Test successes.
- The modifier is capped by the spell's Force.
