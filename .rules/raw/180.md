## Spell Pool Refresh

- Spell Pool refreshes to its full value at the beginning of each Combat Turn.
- Spell Pool dice may be added to Drain Resistance Tests, but the number of Spell Pool dice used cannot exceed the magician's base Sorcery skill.

## Limited Spells

- A magician can apply limits when learning a spell to make the spell easier to handle.
- A fetish-limited spell requires an enchanted, reusable spell fetish to cast.
- Spell fetishes are specific to one spell category: Combat, Detection, Health, Illusion, or Manipulation.
- A spell fetish must be attuned to the magician and to the specific spell during the spell learning process.
- The magician must maintain physical contact with the spell fetish to cast the associated fetish-limited spell.
- A fetish limit applies a -1 modifier either to the Karma cost and difficulty of learning the spell or to the spell's Force for Drain calculation, depending on how the limit is applied.
- If the fetish is lost, the magician cannot cast the limited version of that spell until a replacement is attuned.
- Attuning a replacement fetish requires meditation for a number of hours equal to the spell's Force.
- An exclusive-limited spell makes casting or sustaining the spell an Exclusive Action.
- An exclusive-limited spell applies a -2 modifier to Force for Drain calculation.
