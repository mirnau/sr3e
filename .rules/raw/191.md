## Sustaining Foci

- A sustaining focus maintains a sustained spell without requiring the caster's concentration.
- To use a sustaining focus, the caster must cast the spell into the focus with an Exclusive Complex Action.
- The sustaining focus must be in physical contact with the target when the spell is cast into it.
- A sustaining focus cannot maintain a spell whose Force exceeds the focus's Force.

## Focus Tracking

- A bonded focus contains the owner's astral signature.
- A bonded focus can be used to track its owner through astral space.

## Spell Drain Level

- The base Drain damage level is determined by the spell being cast.
- Combat spell Drain Level is typically equal to the Damage Level chosen for the spell effect.
- If modifiers would raise Drain Level beyond Deadly, each additional level adds +2 to Drain Power instead.

## Spell Drain Examples

- Manabolt Drain: Force / 2 Power against the chosen Damage Level.
- Manaball Drain: Force / 2 Power against the chosen Damage Level + 1.
- Powerball Drain: Force / 2 + 1 Power against the chosen Damage Level + 1.
- Stunbolt Drain: Force / 2 - 1 Power against the chosen Damage Level.
