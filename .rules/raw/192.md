## Detection Spells

- Detection spells can grant the subject enhanced senses or entirely new senses.
- Detection spell senses can include detecting life, magic, or enemies.

## Detection Range

- Detection spell sensory range equals Force multiplied by the caster's Magic Attribute, in meters.
- Example: a Force 5 detection spell cast by a magician with Magic 6 has a range of 30 meters.
