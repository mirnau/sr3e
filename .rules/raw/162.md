## Drain

- Drain represents the mental and physical fatigue a magician suffers when channeling mana.
- A spell's Drain Code consists of a Power Rating and a Damage Level.
- Drain Power is the target number for the Drain Resistance Test.
- Drain mechanics are the same in and out of combat, though combat uses strict sequence timing.
- Drain is usually Stun damage.
- Drain becomes Physical damage when the spell or spirit Force exceeds the caster's Magic Attribute.
- Drain becomes Physical damage when the caster is astrally projecting.
- Every two successes on a Drain Resistance Test reduce Drain damage by one level.
- Drain Power equals half the spell's Force, rounded down, plus the spell modifier.
- Each active spell sustained by the caster adds +2 to the Power of the Drain for any new magic performed.
