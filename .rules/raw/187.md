## Conjuring Drain

- The caster rolls Charisma dice to resist Drain from summoning.
- The Conjuring Drain Resistance Test target number equals the summoned spirit's Force.
- Conjuring Drain damage level is determined by comparing the spirit's Force to the summoner's Charisma.
- Conjuring Drain damage is Light when spirit Force is half the summoner's Charisma or less.
- Conjuring Drain damage is Moderate when spirit Force is greater than half the summoner's Charisma and less than or equal to the summoner's Charisma.
- Conjuring Drain damage is Serious when spirit Force is greater than the summoner's Charisma and less than or equal to 1.5 times the summoner's Charisma.
- Conjuring Drain damage is Deadly when spirit Force is greater than 1.5 times the summoner's Charisma.
