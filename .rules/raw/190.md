## Focus Activation for Spellcasting

- A focus must be bonded to the caster before it can be used.
- A focus must be activated with a Simple Action before it can be used.
- An active focus provides extra dice equal to its Force for the magical tests allowed by its type.
- Bonding a focus impresses the owner's astral signature onto the item.
- Bonding a focus requires a magical ritual lasting hours equal to the focus's Force.
- Bonding a focus requires no special materials.
- At the end of the bonding ritual, the character spends Good Karma according to focus type and Force.
- Only one person can be bonded to a focus at one time.
- Re-bonding a focus to a new owner automatically severs the previous owner's bond.

## Expendable Spell Foci

- An expendable spell focus provides Force dice for either the Sorcery Test or the Drain Resistance Test of a single spell in its specific category.
- An expendable spell focus is consumed after one use.

## Specific Spell Foci

- A specific spell focus provides dice for the Sorcery Test or Drain Resistance Test of one specific spell.
- The caster can split the focus's Force dice between the Sorcery Test and Drain Resistance Test as desired.

## Spell Category Foci

- A spell category focus provides dice for Sorcery, Drain, Spell Defense, or Dispelling for all spells in its chosen category.

## Spirit Foci

- A spirit focus provides Force dice for summoning, controlling, or banishing one type of spirit.
- A spirit focus's dice can be divided between Conjuring and Drain Resistance tests.

## Power Foci

- A power focus increases the caster's Magic Attribute by its Force.
- A power focus increases Magic Attribute by its Force for determining whether Drain is Stun or Physical.
- A power focus increases Magic Attribute by its Force for determining maximum spirit Force.
- A power focus provides Force dice for Sorcery, Conjuring, Drain, or Spell Defense tests.

## Weapon Foci

- A weapon focus is a magical melee weapon.
- A weapon focus adds its Force to the owner's combat skill.
- A weapon focus inflicts its base damage in physical combat.
- A weapon focus inflicts its base damage in astral combat.
- A weapon focus can strike spirits despite Immunity to Normal Weapons.
- In astral combat, a weapon focus's Power is based on Charisma rather than Strength.

## Focus Bonding Costs

- Expendable spell focus bonding cost: 0 Good Karma.
- Specific spell focus bonding cost: 1 x Force Good Karma.
- Sustaining focus bonding cost: 1 x Force Good Karma.
- Spirit focus bonding cost: 2 x Force Good Karma.
- Spell category focus bonding cost: 3 x Force Good Karma.
- Power focus bonding cost: 5 x Force Good Karma.
- Weapon focus bonding cost: (3 + Reach) x Force Good Karma.

## Active Focus Constraints

- A character can have active foci up to their Intelligence rating.
- A focus must be on the owner's person to remain active.
- A focus deactivates immediately if dropped or snatched away.
- Active foci are dual-natured and exist on both the physical and astral planes.
- Dice provided by a focus are used for a test and refresh at the beginning of the next Combat Turn like a dice pool.
