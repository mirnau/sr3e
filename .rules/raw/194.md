## Permanent Spell Time Reduction

- Permanent spells such as Heal, Treat, and Stabilize have a base casting time that Sorcery Test successes divide down.
- Formula: actual time = base time / successes.
- Early successes cut time dramatically; each additional success beyond that gives diminishing returns.
